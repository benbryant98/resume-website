About Me

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me! My full name is Benjamin Curtis Bryant, middle name courtesy of my father, and I am happily married to my best friend. My life so far can be described as a series of forays into the vast unknowns of the universe. Some that were as trivial as learning the proper warding mechanics for Dota 2, others that serve more of a purpose, like understanding what truly makes a perfect char-grilled burger. Essentially, whenever my interest in a topic is piqued, I usually spend a few weeks to months slightly obsessing over that topic until I move on to a new one. It's not an efficient way to go about life, but it certainly gives me a diverse range of conversation starters. With so much change in my life, I am always grateful for my constants. My wife is an amazing person, an extremely intelligent anthropologist, and my fiercest champion. I have a wonderful family who gave me all the tools I needed to be who I am today. Not to mention, I have quite possibly the cutest cats in the world, which you will see many pictures of if you scroll down. Please feel free to check out more about me down below, send me a message, and have a great day!
